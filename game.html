Adventure Game

Enter your name to begin! You were walking on the street with your friend when you were drugged and abducted. You wake up alone after 3 days to find yourself inside an abandoned house. Find your friend in a room named 'secret' to win. Keep in mind, when you want to fight a monster, type in the action fightin followed by the room with the monster inside.
